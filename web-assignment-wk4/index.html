About ACAPAT TECH

ACAPAT TECH is a leading software training and development firm with a mission to empower people and businesses through technology. We provide cutting-edge training programmes in fields such as software engineering, data science, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.

Beyond training, we deliver enterprise-grade software development services—helping startups, SMEs, and large organizations build scalable applications, adopt cloud solutions, and implement secure IT practices.

Our vision is to bridge the global tech talent gap by raising the next wave of world-class African developers and innovators.
